Open'er Festival

Gdynia
June 28-July 1, 2023

It is one of the biggest annual music festivals in Poland. There were already nineteen editions of the festival. The main organizer of the festival is the concert agency Alter Art. Open'er Festival won multiple awards, including the Best Major Festival prize at the European Festivals Awards ceremony in 2009, 2010 and 2019. The festival always features important artists who never visited Poland before, and often played there just once: Prince, Coldplay, The Strokes, Snoop Dogg, The White Stripes, Kanye West, Muse, Kings Of Leon, The Chemical Brothers and Justice.

Previous editions also included: The Killers, Imagine Dragons, Twenty One Pilots, Lana Del Rey, Swedish House Mafia, Travis Scott, Depeche Mode, Bruno Mars, Post Malone, Radiohead, The Weeknd, Florence + The Machine, and many more…

2023 line-up so far: Arctic Monkeys, Lizzo, One Republic

Orange Warsaw Festival

Warsaw
June 2-3, 2023

The first edition of the festival took place in 2008 and since then it is organized annualy by the Alter Art concert agency. It takes place at Służewiec Racing Track. Orange Warsaw Festival is the most important musical and cultural event of Warsaw. Each edition brings to Poland artists that have never performed here before and those that are already loved by the festival audience. You can expect incredibly talented artists of the young generation, pop icons, and rock giants on the OWF stage.

Previous editions included: Tyler, The Creator, Miley Cyrus, Marshmello, Kodaline, Years & Years, Martin Garrix, Lana Del Rey, Skrillex, Dua Lipa, Beyonce, Linkin Park, The Prodigy, and many more…

2023 line-up so far: The 1975, Sam Smith

Kraków Live Festival

Kraków
August 19-20, 2022

Kraków Live Festival is a two-day music festival based in Kraków and promoted by Alter Art. Each year two stages are built on the area destined for the festival, next to the stages there are created: gastronomic area and an area with non-music attractions. The festival line-up is based on the worldwide famous artists as well as on the selected group of Polish artists. Each year the festival draws the attention of tens of thousands of music fans and is a kind of closure for the festival season in Poland. KLF puts emphasis on a non-music festival attractions which enables the festival goers to use the broad cultural offer of the Kraków city. Each year a cooperation is planned with the local culture institutions such as: National Museum in Kraków, MOCAK – Modern Art Museum, Historic Museum.

Previous editions included: Halsey, Lewis Capaldi, Future, Kendrick Lamar, Calvin Harris, Macklemore, DJ Snake, Wiz Khalifa, Ellie Goulding, The Neighbourhood, Sia, Placebo, 30 Seconds to Mars, and many more…
